I've been working on something that started as a completely personal passion project — and it's honestly been one of the most exciting and challenging things I've ever done.

The catch? I had never made a game before in my life. Not even close. The whole thing was genuinely intimidating when I started staring at a blank project. Fast forward to recently: I found out that as a university student, I have access to GitHub Copilot Pro — completely free. That changed everything. Still a lot of road ahead, but I'm proud of where it's going.
